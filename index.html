Hotline Update          23-03-2026
Hotline department colleagues have exactly the same access that we have.

In such a case, why to share hotline number to our customers ?
To achieve the target of 74% Solution rate, the objective should be to resolve all queries and not to give the customer a chance to come back for the same concern.

Well, this is exactly what customer service is, right ?

Note: Hotline numbers or any links should never be shared with the customers as it can lead to an escalation.
Account Hacked          23-03-2026
How to deal with customers whose account was hacked ?

In such cases, do not make any changes in the contract i.e. do not try to resolve the concern immediately.
Simply ask the customers to send an E-Mail to betrugsbearbeitung@sky.de stating the exact issue.

This backend team will be prioritizing such cases and will respond to the customers with the correct resolution.
